Annual Leave:
Employees in the Netherlands are entitled to a minimum of 20 days of annual leave per year.
Public holidays are not counted as part of annual leave.
Unused leave can be carried forward for up to 6 months.

Sick Leave:
There is no fixed limit for sick leave.
Employees must inform their manager on the first day of illness.
Long-term sickness is handled as per Dutch employment regulations.

Parental Leave:
Employees are eligible for parental leave as per Netherlands labor laws.

Approval Process:
Annual leave must be approved by the manager.
Sick leave does not require prior approval but must be reported immediately.
