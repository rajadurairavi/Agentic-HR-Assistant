Annual Leave:
Employees in India are entitled to 24 days of annual leave per calendar year.
Annual leave can be accumulated up to a maximum of 45 days.
Unused annual leave beyond the limit will lapse at the end of the year.

Sick Leave:
Employees are entitled to 12 days of sick leave per year.
Medical certificate is required if sick leave exceeds 2 consecutive days.

Casual Leave:
Employees are entitled to 10 days of casual leave per year.
Casual leave cannot be carried forward.

Maternity Leave:
Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of paid maternity leave as per Indian labor laws.

Approval Process:
All leaves must be applied through the HR portal and approved by the reporting manager.
